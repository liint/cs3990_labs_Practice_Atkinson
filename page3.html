Warhammer: 40000

Warhammer: 40000 is a sci-fi tabletop game that has expanded to many different mediums of entertainment.
Warhammer: 40000, aka Warhammer: 40k, is a tabletop rpg that involves defeating an opponent with a personally constructed army of plastic models.
